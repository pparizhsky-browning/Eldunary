Founded in **Year 780** by ex-Pegasus members specifically to destroy Pegasus — created in direct response to the internal corruption that collapsed the original organization.

A crime syndicate in method but ideological in origin. The ongoing war with Pegasus produces strikes almost weekly.

Leadership conducts covert meetings in Anehull (Kingdom of Vacron). Maintains HQ in Uyrand (Kingdom of Iyhago). Provides protection to the neutral forge in Clocester.
