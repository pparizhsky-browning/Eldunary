King of the Goldhelm Kingdom. Resides in Ovodon. Head of the Goldhelm Family. Ideology: Technocratic Isolationism.

**Personality:** A protective father and proud ruler. Aldric governs through the Goldhelm ideology of technocratic isolationism and racial superiority. He is fiercely protective of his daughter Seraphina and his kingdom, willing to mobilize the full resources of the Goldhelm military when either is threatened.

**Government:** Rules with a council of advisors from each sector: Scientific, Military, Maritime, Merchants Guild, Northern Criozevan Diplomat, and Southern Criozevan Diplomat. His word is final on matters of state.
