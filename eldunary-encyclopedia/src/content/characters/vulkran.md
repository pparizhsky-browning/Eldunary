**Vulkran** was the ruler of the Fire District of Fodon. A knight of molten steel and volcanic power, he wielded a lava-blade and obsidian shield, with a burning eye-core at his chest. His armor could melt sand into glass.

He was slain by **Lucifer (#6 Arashi)** during the collapse of the Warring Districts. His remains lie entombed in a fire-cracked canyon near Plodrough — still warm to this day. Born in Zhuiburn.
