The most widespread race in Criozevan. Evolved from humans during the Crimson Convergence. Found in Vacron, Frada, Akison, Oredsy, and mixed kingdoms.

**Culture:** Culturally adaptive. Common traditions include oral epic chants and oath feasts. Clan names or professions often denote status.

**Social Structure:** Varies by kingdom.

**Magic / Tech:** Balanced use of magi and tech. Often bridge gaps between races in diplomacy.

**Combat:** Region-dependent: swordsmanship in Oredsy, tech arms in Frada. Generally adaptable.

**Relations:** Serve as diplomats, commoners, elites, rebels, and mercenaries. Known for rapid social mobility.

**Religion:** Varies by kingdom.

**Language:** Common tongue (regional variations).

**Status:** STABLE — Threats: cultural fragmentation.
