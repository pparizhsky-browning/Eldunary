Nomadic raiders turned mercantile fleet lords. Once worshipped as sea gods by islanders. Present in Glaril, Neruvalis, Ipadora, and Akison.

Features: cartilage armor, serrated teeth, dorsal fins, gill slits. Extreme affinity for blood — contact causes frenzy state.

**Culture:** Honor-by-hunt society — trophies of kills used in diplomacy. Clan rituals involve blood tides and storm feasts.

**Social Structure:** Clan-ships ruled by Captains. Elders serve as storm-priests.

**Magic / Tech:** Surge magic (linked to currents). Weapon-forged teeth and sonar-sculpted blades.

**Combat:** Blitz tactics, underwater ramming. Bite combat, trident barrages, water drill spirals.

**Relations:** Feared by merchants, known for unpredictable loyalties. Tense truce with Ipadoras, unofficial rivalry.

**Religion:** Worship the Crimson Fin, deity of storms and prey.

**Language:** Ka’torr (guttural, gesture-based; used underwater).

**Status:** SCATTERED, dominant in certain sea zones — Threats: coral scarcity, tech piracy wars.
