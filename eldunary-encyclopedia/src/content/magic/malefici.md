**Malefici** are magic users comprising 27.5% of all Magi. They are split into three groups:

- **Scarlets** (1% of Malefici / 0.275% of all Magi): Magic users whose powers can run out of control.
- **Dark Malefici** (49.5% of Malefici / ~13.6% of all Magi):
  - **Hell Magic** (2% of Dark Malefici): Used to spawn demons and creatures from hell.
  - **Pure Dark Magic** (98% of Dark Malefici): Can bring people back from the dead or possess others.
- **Mage** (49.5% of Malefici / ~13.6% of all Magi): Can cast any spell or enchantment.

Malefici is banned in the Goldhelm Kingdom.
