**Helm** and **Kaboom** left the Goldhelm research complex in Ibeson at the same time, which the organization's historians and intelligence services have documented as one of the most operationally damaging departures in the Goldhelm Kingdom's research program history — not because either individual's specific knowledge was irreplaceable, but because the combination of what they took with them (Kaboom's Detonation Domain applied to holotech materials, Helm's Strategic Domain applied to holotech engineering patterns) created a capability pair that the Goldhelm program spent years attempting to replicate and has not confirmed success on.

The **Strategic Domain** is an information-processing ability: within a defined area, Helm can observe all physical variables simultaneously and generate real-time optimal tactical analysis, essentially running a complete combat or operational scenario through every derivable pathway while the scenario is still developing. The practical effect is that Helm is never surprised by an event that the available information could have predicted, and the available information is extensive because the Domain's observational intake is total rather than selective.

In the Arashi's operational structure, he provides the analytical layer that translates raw capability into directed outcome. Not every Arashi member needs strategic analysis — Dark Paladin's Death Eye makes individual tactical analysis redundant at his level. But for the middle and lower ranks, Helm's assessment of optimal approach to a given objective is the Arashi's closest equivalent to an organizational command intelligence function.

His rank at #12 is lower than his operational impact suggests, which is a reflection of the Arashi's power-primary ranking system — the Strategic Domain is extraordinary in application but does not produce the direct physical threat output that the ranking system weights. He does not appear to find the ranking assessment unjust. Strategy has always worked better from a position where it is underestimated.
