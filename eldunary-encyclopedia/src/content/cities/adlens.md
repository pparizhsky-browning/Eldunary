Diverse, peaceful town in the Warring Darkmane Realm. Home to dark elves who reject violence, Golems who defected from the Golem King, and Goldhelms who fled corruption. Large port. Trade hub from GK to the southeast by boat. Peace treaty signed here. Many rules enforced regularly by the Aces, hired by the local government.
