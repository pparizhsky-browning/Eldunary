Largest city in Criozevan. Located at the geographic center of the continent. Known for its towering administrative buildings and bustling marketplaces. A key location for law, politics, and power deals. Trade and commerce hub. Extremely strict law enforcement. Friendly to outsiders. Crimson Rooks HQ and the Crimson Knight's residence.
