Former mining colony in the Kingdom of Vacron. Known for crystal caverns and unstable ground (frequent collapses from underground duels). Tunnel access to KoF, RoF, and KoI borders. Mixed population of refugees and runaways. Home to a neutral forge with Rose protection. Criminal meeting ground. Central spot between Vacron, Sloumont, and Hiypolis. Town militia uses salvaged war-tech.
